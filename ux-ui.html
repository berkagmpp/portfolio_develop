The HAUKAY Design Process

• UX STRATEGY

ISSUE:   Uncomfortable and difficult systems

CityGym Membership Management Application

In July 2021, I designed City Gym membership management web application which has funtion of register, search, modify, auto calculate for membership, and register, modify, select additional, extra pay for the fitness class.

• UX STRATEGY

ISSUE:   Manual entry, complicated calulation of option cost and total payment price

IMPROVEMENT:   Display of intuitive and detailed option price and total price following the user's selection

ISSUE:   Unaccustomed and uncomfortable with traditional applications with one-sided input methods

IMPROVEMENT:   Improved convenience with diversification of search options and two-way transitions

RESULT:   Easily accessible to staff who is unfamiliar with web applications or even new staff
